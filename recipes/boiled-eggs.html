Boiled Eggs

Scrumptiously seasoned eggs, perfect for a sunday brunch

Ingredients:

  • Barn raised eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Retrieve egg from carton
  2. Place egg in saucepan
  3. Submerge egg in water (completely)
  4. Place saucepan over high heat and bring to boil
  5. Remove egg from water and hit on countertop
  6. If egg breaks, start from step 1 and increase cooking duration
